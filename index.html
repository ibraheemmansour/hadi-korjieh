About Me

My name is Hadi Korjieh, and fitness is not just my profession—it's my passion and way of life.

With over a decade of experience in strength and conditioning, I've dedicated my career to helping individuals unlock their true potential. Whether you're taking your first steps into fitness or you're an athlete looking to break through plateaus, I provide the guidance, structure, and accountability needed to achieve real, lasting results.

My coaching philosophy is built on three core pillars:

  • Discipline: Success in fitness requires consistency and commitment. I teach you to show up, even when motivation fades.
  • Science-Based Training: Every program I design is rooted in exercise science and tailored to your unique goals, body, and lifestyle.
  • Holistic Transformation: True change goes beyond the gym. I help you build sustainable habits that transform your mindset, nutrition, and overall well-being.

I believe that everyone has the power to become the strongest version of themselves—both physically and mentally. Let's make it happen together.
